Phil Schanely

Designer/Developer

Phil received his Master of Fine Arts (M.F.A.) from the Savannah College of Art and Design in 2017. Before coming to Mile Two as a Designer/Developer in 2017 he worked for 10 years in Higher Education at Cedarville University, serving as a staff member in an instructional solutions unit and as a professor in Visual Communication Design.

Phil loves solving complex interactive problems that help make life simpler for users and thrives in the hybrid roles of designer and developer. No solution is “finished” for Phil unless it is both functional and beautiful.

Phil lives in Cedarville, Ohio with his wife and two children. In his spare time he enjoys hiking, jogging, trying new restaurants, and playing video games.
